Welcome to Wellness & Care 🌿

Wellness & Care is a web-based mood tracking application designed to support emotional well-being. It helps users log their feelings, visualize mood trends, and receive meaningful suggestions based on their current state of mind.

  • 🎭 Emoji-based daily mood entry system
  • 📈 Mood visualization with charts
  • 💡 Suggestions tailored to each emotion
  • 🔒 Secure login and registration system
  • 📊 Mood history log for self-reflection

Whether you're feeling great or just okay, this app encourages you to pause and reflect on your emotions. Wellness & Care empowers you to make positive choices and maintain emotional balance.
