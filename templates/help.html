🧠 Frequently Asked Questions

  • 🔐 How do I register?
    Visit the Register page to create your free account.
  • 😊 How do I track my mood?
    Once logged in, use the emoji buttons on the dashboard to log your mood instantly.
  • 📊 Can I see my mood history?
    Yes, click on “Mood Log” to view your emotional trends over time.
  • 🚪 How do I logout?
    Use the "Logout" option in the top menu to securely exit your session.
  • 🔒 Is my data safe?
    Absolutely. We store your mood logs securely and only you can access them.
